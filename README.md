## About Each Project

### Fintech Collection Engine
A merchant payment collection platform supporting five distinct collection methods — UPI, QR,
Virtual Account (VAM), Payment Link, and Manual Deposit — with full commercial/GST calculation,
ledger tracking, and settlement reconciliation across every transaction type.

### Fintech Payout Engine
An outbound fund-transfer platform enabling merchants to pay beneficiaries via IMPS, NEFT, and
RTGS, with a beneficiary approval workflow, bulk payout support with per-item status reporting,
and retry logic designed around idempotency to prevent duplicate transfers.

### Fintech Connected Banking Platform
A self-service, API-driven multi-bank account management platform that lets businesses link,
verify, and monitor multiple bank accounts, and transact against them, through a single unified
dashboard and API — including a dedicated fee wallet model that never touches customer funds.

### BBPS Bill Payment Platform
A Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS) integration enabling end users to discover billers, fetch
live outstanding bill amounts in real time, and pay utility and service bills through one
standardized interface, with built-in protection against stale-amount charging.

### Reseller Management Platform
A partner/reseller dashboard that lets resellers onboard and manage their own portfolio of
merchants under the platform in exchange for a revenue share, built around strict multi-tenant
data isolation as its core testing and architectural principle.

### Healthcare Insurance Platform
A SaaS-based health insurance application managing the complete policy lifecycle — enrollment,
claims processing, billing, and provider network management — across four key entities:
Providers, Payers, Employers, and Members/Patients.

### Travel Marketplace Platform
A dynamic online travel booking marketplace for domestic and international tour packages,
featuring customized packages, price comparison, holiday categories, search filters, and secure
payment integration across web and mobile.

### HRMS Platform
A comprehensive Human Resource Management System streamlining payroll compliance (PF, ESI, TDS),
attendance, leave, recruitment, performance management, onboarding workflows, and biometric
integration with role-based access control.

### LMS Platform
A scalable, multi-tenant Learning Management System for universities, trainers, and educators,
featuring course management, mock tests, certifications, analytics, payment integration, and
live-class collaboration (Zoom/Teams-style integration).

### YOBO — Your Only Banking Option
A consent-based Account Aggregator banking platform (similar in concept to services like Cred or
Finvu), enabling users to securely aggregate, view, and share their financial data across
multiple banks and financial institutions in real-time.

### AI Dispute Resolution Engine
The shared, AI-powered support and dispute resolution layer common across Collection, Payout,
Connected Banking, BBPS, and YOBO — rather than a separate chatbot per product. Reduces average
ticket resolution time from a 24–72 hour baseline to under 6 hours, with ~80% of issues resolved
directly by the AI agent across transaction status, email/mobile number changes, merchant
onboarding, and general fintech Q&A.
